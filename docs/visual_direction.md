\# Lisa Visual Direction



Lisa should not look like a normal desktop app or chatbot.



Lisa should feel like a living AI presence on the desktop: a futuristic, polished, JARVIS-like local AI companion with a unique identity.



\## Core Visual Identity



Lisa’s default visual body is an always-available animated Orb.



The Orb should feel like:



\- a glowing neural sphere;

\- a blue/cyan energy core;

\- animated circular HUD rings;

\- subtle waveform/listening animation;

\- dark futuristic interface;

\- polished desktop command center;

\- alive, responsive, and intelligent;

\- better than common TikTok/JARVIS-style AI orb examples;

\- unique to Lisa, not a direct copy of existing designs.



\## Orb States



The Orb must visually support these states:



\- Idle: subtle glow, low movement.

\- Listening: waveform pulse.

\- Thinking: rotating rings / neural activity.

\- Speaking: voice-synced waveform style.

\- Acting: progress ring / active operation indicator.

\- Waiting Approval: distinct approval pulse/card.

\- Paused: stable paused marker.

\- Error: warning/recovery animation.

\- Emergency Stopped: clear stopped/safe visual state.



\## Interface Direction



Lisa should have:



\- small Orb mode;

\- expanded HUD mode;

\- full command center mode;

\- mission timeline;

\- approval cards;

\- local runtime status;

\- audit/event log;

\- mode indicator;

\- emergency stop button.



\## Design Rules



Do not make Lisa look like:



\- a basic chatbot;

\- a normal admin dashboard;

\- a plain settings app;

\- a web form;

\- a copied JARVIS screen;

\- a copied TikTok orb animation.



Lisa should feel futuristic, personal, local, powerful, and controlled.
